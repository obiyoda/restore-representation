Restore Representation

Revolutionizing American Governance

Introduction

Imagine a nation where every citizen’s voice is truly heard in Congress. “Restore Representation” advocates for one representative for every 30,000 people. Through grassroots crowdfunding, we will hire lobbyists to represent the people’s interests directly in Washington, D.C. By mimicking the election process and promoting local unity over partisanship, we aim to restore a true representative republic and accountability in our government. Join us to bring real change and representation back to Congress.

Manifesto

  • Restore representation: 1 representative for every 30,000 people.
  • Act without waiting for Congress to reduce their power.
  • Use lobbyists to represent the people.
  • Mimic the election process for real U.S. representation.
  • Promote dialogue over division.
  • Encourage local unity over partisanship.

Goals

  • Establish an organization:
    • Complete legal paperwork.
    • Raise initial funds.
  • Fundraise to send one lobbyist to Washington, D.C., representing an initial area.
